FAQ

Quick, clear answers about how Tipza works.

What is the primary purpose of Tipza?

Tipza helps service teams split tips fairly and consistently. Enter the total, pick a method, and get a clear breakdown for each person.

How does Tipza calculate a "fair" split?

Fair depends on your team’s rules. Tipza supports equal, hours, points, and custom weights—and applies the same method consistently every time.

Does Tipza handle payments or move money?

No. Tipza is a calculator and record keeper. It shows the split—payouts happen outside the app based on your workplace process.

Can I share the results with my team?

Yes. After you calculate a split, you can share the breakdown so everyone sees the same numbers.

Why does Tipza use Google Sign-In?

It helps you sign in quickly and keep your data available across devices. Tipza only requests what’s needed for authentication and basic profile info.
