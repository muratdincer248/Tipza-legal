FAQ

Quick answers for reviewers and users.

What is the primary purpose of Tipza?

Tipza’s primary purpose is to help service teams split tip pools fairly and transparently. Users enter a total tip amount, choose a split method (equal, hours, points, or weights), and Tipza calculates each person’s share.

How does Tipza calculate a “fair” split?

“Fair” depends on your team’s rules. Tipza supports common policies (equal split, by hours, by points, or custom weights) and applies the chosen method consistently to the inputs you provide.

Does Tipza handle payments or move money?

Tipza is designed to calculate and present the breakdown. If your workflow includes payouts, they happen outside Tipza according to your team’s process.

Why does Tipza use Google Sign-In?

Google Sign-In can be used to provide a fast, secure way to sign in and keep your account consistent across devices. Tipza requests only the permissions needed for authentication and basic profile identification.
